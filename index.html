Frequently asked

Questions before you start.

Everything you need to know before connecting your bank.

Is my bank data secure?
Yes. WealthRx never sees or stores your bank login — those are handled entirely by Plaid, the same service used by Venmo, Robinhood, and Coinbase. Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We only read transaction data, never move money.
Does WealthRx replace my CPA?
No — WealthRx handles the bookkeeping and deduction tracking (about 80% of what most people pay their CPA for). Your CPA still files your taxes, but with WealthRx they need far less time because your books are already organized. Most customers save $300–$600/month by using WealthRx alongside their CPA.
How accurate is the AI categorization?
WealthRx uses Claude by Anthropic combined with a detailed IRS rules engine. For clear expenses (software, office supplies, travel), accuracy is near 100%. For ambiguous transactions like restaurants or Amazon, WealthRx flags them for your review rather than guessing. Every decision includes an IRS publication reference you can verify.
Which banks are supported?
WealthRx supports over 11,000 financial institutions through Plaid, including Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Citibank, Capital One, Ally, Mercury, Bluevine, Relay, Novo, and most credit unions. If your bank isn't supported, email us and we'll work on adding it.
Can WealthRx file my taxes for me?
No. WealthRx is a financial organization tool — we don't file tax returns. We prepare your data in a CPA-ready format (Excel or PDF) that your accountant can use directly, or that you can reference when filing through services like TurboTax.
What if I have mixed personal and business expenses in one account?
WealthRx handles this. During onboarding, you tell us if your account is business-only or mixed. For mixed accounts, WealthRx flags more transactions for your review so you can separate personal from business. Over time, the system learns your patterns.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings and you'll keep access through the end of your billing period.
Do I need to be a tax expert to use it?
Not at all. WealthRx is built for people who want their finances handled without becoming accountants. The AI does the heavy lifting, flags anything ambiguous, and explains every decision in plain English.
Is there a free trial?
During early access (first 50 customers), WealthRx is free. After launch, we'll offer a 14-day free trial on both plans. Early access members get locked-in pricing as long as they remain subscribed.
